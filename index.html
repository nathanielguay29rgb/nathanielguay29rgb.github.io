Vacations

Maine

Every Summer, my family and I go to Maine to see my dad's side of the family. We also eat really good seafood. When we land at the Boston airport, our favorite restaurant is called Legal Seafoods. We always order clam chowder and lobster rolls, although my dad always makes lobster and steamers (boiled clams) later on in the trip and invites his parents (my grandparents). Sometimes, we also stay in Boston for a few days, and it is so interesting to see the Revolutionary War history and memorials.

Hawaii

Although we do not go to Hawaii as often as Maine, my family still loves traveling here. The island we always go to is Maui. Our favorite restaurants are called Monkeypod and Pita Paradise. Besides the food, we also love to swim in the ocean (the water is much warmer in Hawaii than in Maine).

Thailand

During the winter of 2023, my family and I traveled to Thailand. It was such a unique experience, and the food was great. We saw so many cool temples and old architecture from the Khmer Empire, and also learned about Thailands involvement in historcal events such as WWll. We were even able to witness the religious depth of the country, because our tour group interacted with Buddhist monks at a monastery.
