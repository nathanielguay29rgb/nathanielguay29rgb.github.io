Fishkeeping/Aquascaping

Right now, I am building a 16 gallon community aquarium.

  1. Filter: Aquarium Co-Op Medium Easy Flow Sponge Filter, course porrosity (25ppi).
  2. Heater: 75 watt Eheim Jager heater.
  3. Substrate: Fluval Stratum
  4. Thermometer: Zacro LCD Digital Thermometer
  5. Airline tubing: Penn-Plax Airline Tubing
  6. Air pump: Aquarium Co-Op Air Pump with Battery Backup
  7. Check valve: Aquarium Co-Op check valve
  8. Tank: Landen 60p 16 gallon
  9. Fish: 2 Honey Gauramis, 6 Albino Corydoras, 2 Amano Shrimp
